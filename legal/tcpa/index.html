TCPA Disclosure

Effective: May 1, 2026

1. What you're consenting to

When you submit a form, click a quote-request CTA, or otherwise provide your phone number on UtilityBenefits.com, you consent under the federal Telephone Consumer Protection Act ("TCPA") and applicable state laws to receive:

  • Telephone calls — including pre-recorded and artificial voice messages, and calls placed using an automatic telephone dialing system (autodialer);
  • Text/SMS messages;

at the phone number you provided, from UtilityBenefits.com and from the partner network of program operators, carriers, and suppliers we work with — including for marketing and informational purposes.

2. Consent is not a condition of any service

You are not required to provide this consent in order to receive information about programs you may be eligible for or to obtain a comparison of rates. You can decline by not submitting the form and instead calling us at 1-833-555-0143 or emailing us.

3. Frequency and rates

Message frequency varies. You may receive: an immediate confirmation of your inquiry, calls or texts from a partner who has been matched to your inquiry, and follow-up reminders. Standard message and data rates may apply.

4. How to opt out

  • SMS: Reply STOP to any message to opt out. Reply HELP for help.
  • Calls: Tell the caller you want to be added to the do-not-call list, or email privacy@utilitybenefits.com.
  • All marketing: Email privacy@utilitybenefits.com with the phone number(s) and email(s) you want suppressed. We will honor opt-outs within 10 business days.

5. State-specific notices

Florida. Under the Florida Telephone Solicitation Act, your written consent is required before we (or partners) make telephone solicitations using an automated system. Submitting a form on this site constitutes your written consent under Fla. Stat. § 501.059.

Oklahoma. Similar consumer-protection language applies under the Oklahoma Telephone Solicitation Act.

6. Recording

For training and quality-assurance purposes, calls to and from UtilityBenefits.com may be monitored or recorded as permitted by applicable law.

7. Contact

Questions about this disclosure: privacy@utilitybenefits.com.
