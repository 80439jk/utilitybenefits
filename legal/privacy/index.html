Privacy Policy

Effective: May 1, 2026 · Last updated: May 1, 2026

The short version

We're an information and comparison service. We collect what you tell us (zip code, household size, contact info) plus standard web analytics. We use it to show you which programs and rates apply to your situation, and — if you ask us to — to connect you with a partner program (a Lifeline carrier, an electricity supplier, a Medicaid resource, etc.). We do not sell your personal information to third-party data brokers. We do share contact information with partners you choose to be referred to. You can opt out of marketing contact at any time.

1. What we collect

Information you provide

When you fill out an eligibility form, request a comparison, or call us, we collect: name, email, phone, ZIP code, household size, household income range, household composition, current utility provider(s), and any other field you complete. When you contact our editorial team, we collect what you send us.

Information collected automatically

Standard server logs (IP address, user-agent, referrer), cookies and similar technologies for analytics and ad-attribution (Google Analytics 4, Google Ads conversion pixels, Meta pixel — when active), and click and form-interaction events.

Information from third parties

If you arrive from an ad, we receive standard click identifiers (gclid, fbclid, etc.). If a partner verifies your application, we may receive status updates ("approved," "declined," "needs info"). We do not receive your full government file (Social Security number, etc.) — that is collected directly by the program operator.

2. How we use it

  • To match you with eligible programs and rate options.
  • To deliver results to you (email or on-screen).
  • To pass relevant information to a partner you have asked us to refer you to.
  • To run analytics and improve the site.
  • To detect fraud and abuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Who we share it with

We share information with:

  • Partners you choose to be referred to. If you click "apply" or "switch" on a partner offer, we pass the information necessary to complete the referral. The partner becomes the controller of that data once it is in their system; their privacy policy governs their use of it.
  • Service providers. Hosting (Vercel), analytics (Google), email (a transactional ESP), and similar vendors operating under contract.
  • Legal authorities. When required by valid legal process or to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • An acquirer. If we are part of a merger or sale, information may transfer with the business — subject to this policy.

We do not sell your personal information to third-party data brokers, marketing list vendors, or to anyone whose business is reselling user data.

4. Cookies and tracking

We use first-party cookies (analytics, session) and third-party cookies (Google, Meta) for analytics and ad attribution when you arrive from a paid campaign. Your browser's "Do Not Track" setting and Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals are honored where required by law.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information; to data portability; and to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising. Email privacy@utilitybenefits.com to exercise any of these. California residents see also our Do Not Sell My Info page.

6. Children

The site is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their information.

7. Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No system is perfectly secure; we promptly notify users of breaches as required by law.

8. Changes

We will post material changes here and update the "Last updated" date. If changes are material, we will give reasonable advance notice (e.g., a banner on the site).

9. Contact

Questions: privacy@utilitybenefits.com.
