Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

Effective: May 1, 2026

What we do today

UtilityBenefits.com does not sell your personal information to third-party data brokers in the everyday sense of the word. However, under California's CCPA and CPRA — and parallel laws in Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, Utah, and other states — the term "sale" or "sharing" is defined broadly enough that some of our advertising and partner-referral activities may qualify. Specifically:

  • If you submit an eligibility form and we connect you with a partner program, the transmission of your information to that partner may be considered "sharing" or "sale" under California law.
  • Cookies set by Google Ads, Google Analytics, Meta, or similar advertising platforms may constitute "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Opt out

To opt out of any sale or sharing of your personal information:

  • Use the Global Privacy Control (GPC). If your browser sends a GPC signal, we treat it as a verified opt-out request and apply it automatically.
  • Email us. Send your name, email, and phone number (if you've previously contacted us) to privacy@utilitybenefits.com with subject line "Do Not Sell or Share." We will confirm within 10 business days and complete processing within 15.
  • Disable advertising cookies. Use the cookie-preferences control in our footer to disable advertising and analytics cookies for this browser. (Note: this only affects this browser/device.)

What happens after you opt out

  • We stop sharing your information with advertising platforms for cross-context advertising.
  • If you submit a future form, we will only use your information to deliver the result you requested. We will not pass it to a partner unless you give explicit, separate consent at that point.
  • You will continue to be able to use the site normally; opting out does not change what content you can access.

Other rights you have

California, Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, Utah, and other state residents may also have the right to (1) access the personal information we hold about you, (2) correct inaccurate information, (3) delete your information, (4) limit our use of "sensitive" personal information, and (5) appeal a denial of any of these requests. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Authorized agents

You may submit a request through an authorized agent. We will require written authorization signed by you and we will verify your identity directly before completing the request.
