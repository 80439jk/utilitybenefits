How we choose authors

Our authors are chosen for subject-matter expertise plus editorial judgment about how to communicate it. The combination matters: many people understand LIHEAP rules in detail; far fewer can explain LIHEAP rules to a household that's never applied to a federal program before. We hire for both.

All authors disclose conflicts of interest to the editorial team. Any author with a personal financial relationship to a utility company, assistance program administrator, or competing comparison service must recuse from the relevant coverage. See our full editorial standards for the policies that bind every byline on the site.
